Srila Gurudeva's Audios

Sample text ....

In one informal darsana in Mathura [1994], Srila Gurudeva Sri Srimad Bhaktivedanta Narayana Gosvami Maharaja made one prophecy, “A day will come when I will not be in this world and many will think that all the books [composed by him] are so valuable.” In that same darsana, he also expressed one desire, “There should be a group of very qualified devotees who can take all my lectures and they should organize them and publish them as books. Because I cannot organize, I can only speak.”
